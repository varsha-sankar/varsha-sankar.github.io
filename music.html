Music

 

I have been learning Carnatic vocal Music for about 8 years on an average. My family is into Indian classical music for several generations and my grand uncle is the renowned Mridangam Maestro Guru Karaikudi Mani. I am also a professional Bharatanatyam dancer and have been performing solos and as a part of a group for several years.
